Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a West African dish prepared from rice, spices and other ingredient put together in one pot. The method of preparation differs across the various groups in the West African region. It is very tasty and delicious which makes it one of the likable dish in the region. The above picture is a dish from Ghana.

Majority of Ghanaians like this food because of its nutritional value. It is composed of all the nutrients needed for a healthy living such as carbohydrate, protein, fats, vitamins and more. Method of preparation has been described below but in the meantime, lets look at the various ingredient needed.

Ingredients:

  • rice
  • meat
  • oil
  • chili pepper
  • spices
  • vegetables

Method of Preparation

  1. Pour reasonable oil into pot to fry the meat.
  2. Pour your chopped vegetables into the hot oil after frying the meat.
  3. Add spices to the frying vegetables and small salt.
  4. Add quantifiable amount of water onto the vegetables.
  5. Add rice onto the boiling vegetables.
  6. Regulate the fire to prevent the food from being burnt
  7. Serve the food with the fried meat and leafy.
